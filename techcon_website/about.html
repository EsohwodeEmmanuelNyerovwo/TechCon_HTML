History

TechCon began in 2018 as a small gathering of innovators and developers eager to share their ideas. Over the years, it has grown into a global conference attracting thousands of attendees from around the world.

Mission

Our mission is to empower the tech community by fostering collaboration, sharing knowledge, and inspiring innovation. TechCon provides a platform for industry leaders, developers, and enthusiasts to explore the latest advancements in technology.

Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has hosted some of the brightest minds in technology:

Dr. Uche Chukwu

Dr. Uche Chukwu, an AI researcher, shared her groundbreaking work on ethical machine learning and inspired discussions on the responsible use of artificial intelligence.

James Mathy

James Mathy, a software engineer and entrepreneur, presented his journey of building scalable startups and highlighted the importance of diversity in the tech ecosystem.

Professor Ben Emmy

Professor Ben Emmy discussed advancements in cybersecurity and provided key insights into protecting digital infrastructure in an increasingly connected world.
